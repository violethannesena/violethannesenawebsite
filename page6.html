Violet Battaglia (violin/viola) and Trevor Treglia (electronics/dulcimer) are a duo dedicated to exploring extended-duration performances employing sustained tones and drones within the framework of extended microtonal just intonation. The collaborative effort was born from a shared love of the amalgamation of age-old traditions with experimental methodologies. The project aims to illuminate the intricacies of psychoacoustic phenomena and sonic spatial awareness, cultivating a synthesis between the realms of scientific inquiry, tradition, and experimentation.
